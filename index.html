What is HackRiddle?

HackRiddle is the Embry-Riddle's first 12-hour hackathon with a focus on aviation and aerospace.

group_work

What happens at the hackathon?

Participants spend 12 hours working in teams of 1 to 4 people with a single task: to build something they are excited about. There will be code workshops, programming mentors and food to keep you going along the way.
