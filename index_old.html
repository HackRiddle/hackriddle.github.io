What is HackRiddle?

HackRiddle is the Embry-Riddle's first 12-hour hackathon with a focus on aviation and aerospace.

group_work

What happens at the hackathon?

Participants spend 12 hours working in teams of 1 to 4 people with a single task: to build something they are excited about. There will be code workshops, programming mentors and food to keep you going along the way.

What do most people make?

Whatever you really want to make and are passionate about. Projects can be either hardware or software based.

group_work

Who can attend?

All college and university students are welcome to attend.

settings

How big can a team be?

Maximum team size is four people.

info_outline

How much does it cost to attend?

HackRiddle is absolutely free. Come to build cool things and we will privde the food, location, swag, and prizes!

group_work

What should I bring?

Laptop and charger at minimum. Please bring anything that will help make your projects awesome!

settings

Do you have to know how to code to attend?

No, we will have plenty of resources to help you get started. We will also have some awesome mentors and programs on hand for you to ask questions.
